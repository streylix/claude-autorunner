Wake Word

Always-on voice activation. Say your wake phrase, wait for the chime, then speak your command — after a pause it's transcribed and sent to the manager as a voice memo. Works even when this window is hidden.

Keeps the microphone open and runs a local, offline spotter (Vosk). Nothing leaves your machine until the wake word fires.

When the Discord bot is linked and in a voice channel, ignore the host microphone's wake word so saying the phrase in the room only triggers once (via the bot), not twice. Local wake word works normally when the bot isn't in a call.

The phrase that activates listening. Lowercase, common words work best (the spotter is restricted to exactly this phrase).

How long a pause ends your command and sends it.

Higher = stricter matching and fewer false activations (the spotter must be more confident it heard the phrase). Lower it if your real wake word stops being detected.
